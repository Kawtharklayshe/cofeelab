Coffee Lab presents the new aluminum capsules of its own production!

The well-known varieties of Coffee Lab: Brazil, Guatemala, Ethiopia and Kenya came in aluminum capsules. Now your favorite variety comes to your home in the form of a capsule, with a perfect flow of coffee, thanks to the technology of the “professional machine” located inside the capsule. CoffeeLab is the only chain that has fully verticalized the production of aluminum capsule with continuous testing and great patience in all areas of research, the result of which will surely reward you.

Look for the new aluminum capsules in all Coffee Lab stores! Find your nearest store here: https://coffeelab.gr/en/stores/
