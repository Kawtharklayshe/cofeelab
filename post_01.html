Boutique Chocolat from Coffee Lab’s “Choco Lab”!

Uncategorized / By support

Coffee Lab is no longer just about its high-quality single-variety coffees. New Its era is here and it offers a 360 Experience. With the upgrade of the stores’ image, there was also an upgrade in their range proposals that also have to do with food. She introduced us to her buttery, top quality and original croissants, and now she’s coming for to redefine the absolutely timeless, sweet habit – chocolate! Of course with her own suggestion – Boutique Chocolat. Of course thought out to the last detail – real, handmade and in fine flavors to identify all kinds of chocolate lovers – and not only.

Chocolate pieces in 4 flavors:
milk chocolate filled with praline and hazelnut, milk chocolate filled with white cream and almond, dark chocolate filled with cocoa and almond cream, white chocolate filled with white cream, almond and cranberry and pack mix – for treat or gift, adds a little touch luxury in everyday life.
Chocolate bar in 3 flavors:
milk, dark, white chocolate – three sticks, clean flavors and a small healthy snack for them selected or healthy of the group
Handmade chocolate bar in 4 flavors:
milk chocolate with whole hazelnuts or with whole almonds, dark chocolate with whole almonds and white chocolate with whole almonds and cranberries – for those they are looking for a full, rich chocolate experience.
Milk chocolate or white chocolate drops,
in a small glass, for those who are satisfied with a small one chocolate awakening.
Jar of spreads in 3 flavors:
hazelnut praline, strawberry, pistachio – for those who want to start the their day with a coating of luxury.

And finally, the most exciting of all – sachets of chocolate flakes in 6 flavors of which the 3 flavors: chocolate strawberry, salted caramel and chocolate hazelnut – warm on cold winter days and refreshing in summer, the new drink of chocolate accompanies you in the comfort of your home.
