About your grower

Cupping characteristics

Cup score: 74.25

An immigrant family from Italy who went to work on the coffee plantations, the Montanari family has been growing coffee for 4 consecutive generations. Joao Batista Montanari has been in the Cerrado Mineiro area for 30 years. In the family, coffee is their main occupation, while its children are responsible for the improvements in coffee processing, but their first concern is the search for the best quality. In 2015, their farm was the winner of the “Competitiveness Award”, which proves that both he and the whole family has focused on the production of specialty coffee, using sustainable methods and modern management. His son, Marcelo Montanari, explains: “I am a 4th generation producer and a lot has changed since my great-grandfather was in charge. In the old days we were only interested in production. Today we focus on the consumer and what exactly is needed. “This motivated me to focus on both the quality of the coffee and its sustainability.”

About Cerrado Mineiro

Designation of Origin

The Cerrado Mineiro region is world-renowned for the high quality of unique coffee origin in Brazil which is located in the northwest of Minas Gerais State. Having well-defined seasons – hot and humid summer and a pleasant dry winter is a strong feature of the area. Coffee plantations are cultivated in areas with an altitude of 800m to 1300m where it results in high quality coffee with a unique identity. The coffees are of unique origin with guaranteed quality from the area of ​​Cerrado Mineiro – D. O. Board of Regulators.

Our official production process

The production process of the unique origin of coffees emphasizes and evaluates the characteristics of our terroir . Only coffees grown within the officially restricted area that follow the rules of the production process set by our Board of Regulators may have the stamp of guaranteed origin and quality of the Cerrado Mineiro-D.O area.

Farm production must be located within the delimited area of unique origin (cafes)

Minimum Altitude 800m reaching up to 1300m.

Arabica is the official genre

Minimum quality 80pts based on SCA methodology

The use of good practice and respect for the laws of Brazil

Coffee batches should only be stored in recognized cooperatives

Only official coffee bags (packaging) marked with the stamp of guaranteed origin and quality should be used

** terroir = So in theory, terroir is an umbrella term that combines all the different factors that affect wine: the effect of climate, soil, topography, humidity, rainfall, sunshine, soil chemicals but also human intervention in the cultivation of the vineyard when it is done in a traditional way that respects the ecosystem.
